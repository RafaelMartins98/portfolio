About Me

Started over 5 months as a trainee in the system administration sector, at this time I was involved in several internal projects, mostly as server setup, based on pure UNIX or Docker. Some other activities were done, such as internal user helpdesk, starting from clients to company developers, allowing the management of some new technologies like SVN, Bugzilla, Jenkins and additionally Xwiki, for work documentation.

After the trainee time, a junior developer contract has been presented due to my coding experience, having in mind my high school experience and my future ambitious. With over 2 years and 6 months of working as a junior developer, a lot of team work, new technologies have been used in some internal company IT projects, especially in the public sector projects like “Segurança Social”.

By this, I was able to work my teamwork skills, leadership and loyalty, where a new level of healthy pressure was installed while the task was being done. Always showed a motivated and good-humored side looking always for the good side of the problems in order to overcome them.

The most challenging projects I was involved in, have been the ones where I was responsible for all the project management, since architecture, problems corrections, new feature development, all by my own, having in mind every time the client satisfaction, mostly these projects were based on Liferay, Java, JSF, SOAP, REST Web Services and MySQL, being these technologies the main feature of them
